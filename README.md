# My FitPal

> A daily workout tracker that uses a Mongo database with a Mongoose schema and handles routes with Express. Users can create a new workout plan, add exercises to an existing workout plan, and view statistics about previous workouts.

## Usage

1. Open URL https://fitness-tracker-mk-kwon.herokuapp.com/
2. Click the 'New Workout' button
3. Select your exercise and fill in the prompts
4. Add your exercise. Once it's complete, click your 'Complete' button
5. When you hit 'Complete' you will be brought back to the main page to see your progress
6. Click 'Dashboard' tab
7. You can see pie and circle charts on your progress
